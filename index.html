Intro

MarketingDAO kicked off on October 6th at DevconV in Osaka, Japan. It's an initiative by a group of aligned community members to help better communicate Ethereum and Ethereum projects to the world.

“A DAO is basically just a way for a group of people to align around a cause and pool their funds together, knowing absolutely that no one can spend a penny without the DAO Members, including themselves, voting on it first.”
